# Academic Professional Portfolio Scheme
**Email Contact:** TECH.WORKSHOPS@GROKLYGROUP.ME

## Design Philosophy
Warm, scholarly aesthetic designed for education, training, and workshop facilitation. Creates an approachable mentor feel with classic academic authority.

## Color Palette
- **Background:** Warm cream (#FEF9E7), soft beige (#F5F5DC), parchment (#FAF0E6)
- **Primary Accent:** Deep burgundy (#800020), rich burgundy (#8B0000)
- **Secondary Accent:** Forest green (#228B22), sage green (#9CAF88)
- **Tertiary Accent:** Warm brown (#8B4513), muted gold (#B8860B)
- **Text:** Dark brown (#3E2723), warm grey (#6D4C41)

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** Merriweather (serif) - Classic, readable, authoritative
- **Secondary Font:** Lora (serif) - Elegant, warm complement
- **Weight Range:** 400 (regular), 700 (bold), 900 (black)

## Visual Elements
- **Library/Bookshelf Aesthetic:** Warm, inviting backgrounds reminiscent of classic libraries
- **Badge-Style Course Offerings:** Gold gradient badges for featured content
- **Timeline Emphasis:** Academic journey timeline with burgundy accents
- **Testimonial Highlights:** Green-accented testimonial sections
- **Classic Layout:** Traditional academic proportions with modern touches

## Key Features
1. **Warm Color Scheme:** Cream/beige backgrounds with burgundy and forest green
2. **Serif Typography:** Classic Merriweather and Lora fonts
3. **Academic Border Styles:** Subtle borders with classic proportions
4. **Mentor Feel:** Approachable, warm, professional
5. **Workshop Focus:** Educational emphasis throughout design
6. **Teaching Expertise:** Skills section with green accents
7. **Dark Mode:** Warm library at night aesthetic

## Component Styling
- **Navigation:** Burgundy border, cream background, classic serif
- **Hero Section:** Warm gradients, scholarly welcome
- **Project Cards:** Course/workshop cards with burgundy top borders
- **Stats Cards:** Badge-style with gold accents
- **Timeline:** Burgundy centerline with gold accent circles
- **Contact Section:** Workshop inquiry focus with educational icons
