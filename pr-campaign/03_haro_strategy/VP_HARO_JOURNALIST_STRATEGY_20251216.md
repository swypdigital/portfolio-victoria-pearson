## ðŸ“§ PART 1: HARO STRATEGY

### **What is HARO?**

**Help A Reporter Out** (helpareporter.com) connects journalists with expert sources. Journalists post queries 3x daily, you respond with expertise, they feature you in articles.

**Why It Works:**
- FREE (always will be)
- 3 daily emails with 30-60 journalist queries each
- Journalists NEED sources to meet deadlines
- Your unique story = high response rate

### **Setup Instructions (5 Minutes):**

1. Go to: **helpareporter.com**
2. Click "Sign Up as a Source" (NOT "Sign Up as a Journalist")
3. Email: [Your Email]
4. Select Categories:
   - âœ… High Tech
   - âœ… Business & Finance

   - âœ… General (catch-all for diversity stories)
5. Verify email
6. Start receiving queries 3x daily (5:35am, 12:35pm, 5:35pm ET)

### **How to Scan HARO Emails (10 Minutes 3x Daily):**

**Ctrl+F Search for These Keywords:**
- AI (artificial intelligence)
- Machine learning
- Tech career
- Age discrimination
- Career change
- Self-taught

### **HARO Success Metrics:**

**Week 1-2:** Send 10 responses
**Week 3-4:** Send 15 responses (getting faster)
**Week 5+:** Send 5-10/week consistently

**Conversion Rate:**
- 10-20% response rate from journalists (normal)
- 1 placement per 10-20 pitches (realistic)
- First placement often takes 15-30 responses (don't give up!)

### **HARO Pro Tips:**

1. **Speed Matters:** Respond within 2 hours of query going out (journalists work on tight deadlines)
2. **Be Specific:** Generic responses get ignored; specific, quotable insights get used
3. **Include Numbers:** "$20M portfolio" is more compelling than "valuable portfolio"
4. **Offer Photos:** "High-res headshots available upon request" = more likely to use you
5. **Follow Up:** If they don't respond in 48 hours, send ONE polite follow-up
6. **Track Everything:** Spreadsheet of query, journalist, response date, outcome
