## Risk Management

### Technical Risks

**Risk 1: Model Provider Dependency**
- **Threat:** OpenAI/Anthropic raises prices 50%
- **Impact:** Margin compression, price increase needed
- **Mitigation:** MOECraft routes to cheapest model, self-hosted option in Phase 5
- **Contingency:** Open-source LLM fallback (Llama 3, Mistral)

**Risk 2: Scaling Challenges**
- **Threat:** System can't handle 10,000 concurrent users
- **Impact:** Downtime, user churn, reputation damage
- **Mitigation:** Load testing in Phase 3, auto-scaling infrastructure
- **Contingency:** Queue system, rate limiting, capacity planning

**Risk 3: Quality Degradation**
- **Threat:** Generation quality drops below 4.0/5.0
- **Impact:** User dissatisfaction, churn increase
- **Mitigation:** Automated quality monitoring, A/B testing
- **Contingency:** Human review queue, quality guarantees

### Business Risks

**Risk 1: Competitive Pressure**
- **Threat:** Jasper/Copy.ai launch vertical-specific tools
- **Impact:** Market share loss, pricing pressure
- **Mitigation:** Fast iteration, vertical specialization moat
- **Contingency:** Pivot to underserved verticals, enterprise focus

**Risk 2: Slower Growth**
- **Threat:** User growth 50% below projections
- **Impact:** Runway concerns, investor confidence
- **Mitigation:** Aggressive marketing, referral programs
- **Contingency:** Cut burn rate, focus on profitability

**Risk 3: Churn Spike**
- **Threat:** Monthly churn exceeds 10%
- **Impact:** Negative unit economics, growth stall
- **Mitigation:** User feedback loops, retention features
- **Contingency:** Win-back campaigns, pricing adjustments

### Regulatory Risks

**Risk 1: AI Regulation**
- **Threat:** New laws require AI disclosure/watermarking
- **Impact:** Development delays, compliance costs
- **Mitigation:** Monitor legislation, build compliance early
- **Contingency:** Automated watermarking, transparency features

**Risk 2: Copyright Concerns**
- **Threat:** Legal challenges around AI-generated content ownership
- **Impact:** User trust loss, legal costs
- **Mitigation:** Clear ToS, indemnification clauses
- **Contingency:** Legal insurance, copyright guarantees
