## üéØ WHAT IS AN ACQUI-HIRE?

### Definition
**Acqui-hire** = Company acquires your IP + hires you as employee

**Structure:**
- **IP Acquisition:** $500K-1M one-time signing bonus
- **Employment:** $250K-400K annual comp (salary + equity)
- **Total Year 1:** $750K-1.4M

### Why Companies Do Acqui-Hires
1. **Talent acquisition:** Hiring exceptional engineers is hard
2. **IP acquisition:** Get working products instead of building from scratch
3. **Speed to market:** 6-12 months of development time saved
4. **Competitive advantage:** Block competitors from hiring you

### Why This Works for You
- **Immediate cash:** $500K-1M upfront (vs. waiting for SaaS revenue)
- **Job security:** W2 employment with benefits
- **Retain some IP:** License-back rights to GenCraft (never sell the engine)
- **Leverage:** IP gives you negotiating power for salary + equity

## üíº ACQUI-HIRE PACKAGE STRUCTURE

### Standard Components

**1. Signing Bonus (IP Acquisition)**
- Range: $500K-1M
- Purpose: Purchase rights to InterviewPlus, BookCraft, TokenCalc, etc.
- Payment: Lump sum (vs. vesting over time)
- Tax: Treated as ordinary income (plan for 35-40% tax)

**2. Base Salary**
- Range: $180K-250K
- Level: Staff/Principal Engineer
- Standard: Paid biweekly

**3. Equity**
- Range: 0.1%-0.5% (seed/Series A) or $200K-500K RSUs (later-stage)
- Vesting: 4 years, 1-year cliff
- Type: Stock options (early-stage) or RSUs (later-stage)

**4. Relocation (If Applicable)**
- Range: $10K-50K
- Purpose: Moving costs if relocating to SF/NY

**5. Benefits**
- Health insurance (Platinum-level)
- 401(k) match (4-6%)
- Unlimited PTO (or 20-25 days)
