## üìä OUTREACH BEST PRACTICES

### Timing Strategy
**Best Times to Send:**
- **LinkedIn:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am EST (when people check before meetings)
- **Cold Emails:** Monday 9-11am EST (start of week, inbox clearing)
- **Journalist Pitches:** Tuesday-Wednesday 10am-2pm EST (after morning meetings)
- **Follow-Ups:** Thursday afternoons (end of week urgency)

**Avoid:**
- Mondays before 9am (inbox overwhelm)
- Fridays after 3pm (mentally checked out)
- Weekends (unprofessional for cold outreach)

### Personalization Checklist
Before sending ANY template:
- ‚úÖ Replace [Name] with actual first name
- ‚úÖ Replace [Company] with actual company name
- ‚úÖ Add 1-2 sentences showing you researched them
- ‚úÖ Spell-check (use Grammarly)
- ‚úÖ Test links (make sure they work)

### A/B Testing
**Subject Lines:**
- Test 2 versions for every 10 emails
- Track open rates (use Streak for Gmail or MailTrack)
- Winning subject line becomes default

**Email Length:**
- Short (<150 words) for busy execs
- Medium (150-300 words) for hiring managers
- Long (300-500 words) for journalists (they need context)

### Follow-Up Cadence
**Standard 3-Email Sequence:**
- Email 1: Monday, Week 1
- Email 2: Thursday, Week 1 (3 days later)
- Email 3: Tuesday, Week 2 (5 days after Email 2)
- **Stop after Email 3** (don't be a stalker)
